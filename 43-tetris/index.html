Game Rules

• Complete horizontal lines to clear them and earn points

• Clearing multiple lines at once awards bonus points

• Game speeds up as you progress through levels

• Game ends when pieces reach the top of the board

Scoring

1 Line: 100 points

2 Lines: 300 points

3 Lines: 500 points

4 Lines (Tetris): 800 points
